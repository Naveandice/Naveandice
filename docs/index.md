## So, how to pronounce Naveandice?

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Do NOT waste your time practicing pronunciation.
> If you can't say Naveandice, then just call me [Dicey](#you-can-use-a-short-name-instead).

There are two ways to pronounce my username:

- navy-an-dice /'ne&#618;.vi.&#601;n.da&#618;s/
- nave-an-dice /'ne&#618;v.&#601;n.da&#618;s/

> [!NOTE]
> - It's better to use the one that is convenient for you. My favorite is the first one.
> - The misspelling of my username is okay and even welcomed, it's funny.

### You can use a short name instead!

You don't need to practice and master the pronunciation of my username, just call me [Dicey][dicey-dict] instead.

This may be helpful for those who couldn't pronounce Naveandice correctly or didn't even try.
