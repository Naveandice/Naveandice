## So, how to pronounce Naveandice?

My nickname has four <!-- (or two?) --> ways of pronunciation:

- navy-an-dice /'ne&#618;.vi.&#601;n.da&#618;s/ or navy-and-ice /'ne&#618;.vi.&#601;nd.a&#618;s/; and
- nave-an-dice /'ne&#618;v.&#601;n.da&#618;s/ or nave-and-ice /'ne&#618;v.&#601;nd.a&#618;s/.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> You can always use the one that is convenient for you.
